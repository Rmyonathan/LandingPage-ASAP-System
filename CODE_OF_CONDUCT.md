# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race
- Religion, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status, or other characteristics

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being patient and understanding
- Offering help and support to others
- Celebrating diversity and inclusion

### Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate
- Discriminatory language or actions
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Disruptive or off-topic discussions

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

- Email: conduct@tokoglobalteknik.com
- GitHub: Create a private issue
- Phone: +62 123 456 7890

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the report within 7 days
3. **Resolution**: We will take appropriate action within 14 days
4. **Follow-up**: We will follow up to ensure the issue is resolved

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Consequences may include:

- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban
- Removal from project
- Legal action (in extreme cases)

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, email, meetings)
- Public spaces when representing the project
- All contributors and community members
- All project-related activities

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction
- **Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or behavior
- **Action**: A private, written warning
- **Consequence**: Clear explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate

### 2. Warning
- **Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions
- **Action**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior
- **Consequence**: No interaction with the people involved for a specified period

### 3. Temporary Ban
- **Impact**: A serious violation of community standards
- **Action**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction
- **Consequence**: No public or private interaction with the community

### 4. Permanent Ban
- **Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards
- **Action**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction
- **Consequence**: No public or private interaction with the community

## Reporting Guidelines

### What to Include

When reporting, please include:

- Your contact information
- Names of those involved
- When and where the incident occurred
- What happened
- Any additional context
- Any supporting evidence

### What Happens Next

1. We will acknowledge your report
2. We will investigate the incident
3. We will determine appropriate action
4. We will follow up with you
5. We will take steps to prevent recurrence

## Confidentiality

- All reports will be kept confidential
- We will not share your identity without permission
- We will protect your privacy
- We will only share information on a need-to-know basis

## Appeals

If you disagree with a decision:

1. Contact us within 30 days
2. Provide additional information
3. We will review the decision
4. We will provide a final response
